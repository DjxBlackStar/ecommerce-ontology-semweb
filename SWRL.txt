If a product has a rating greater than 4.5,
Then it is a PremiumProduct.
ecomm:hasReview(?r), ecomm:hasRating(?r, ?rate), swrlb:greaterThan(?rate, 4.5), ecomm:hasReview(?p, ?r) 
→ ecomm:PremiumProduct(?p)


If a product has a price greater than 1000,
Then it qualifies for a discount.
ecomm:hasPrice(?p, ?price), swrlb:greaterThan(?price, 1000) 
→ ecomm:EligibleForDiscount(?p)


If a customer placed more than 5 orders,
Then they are a LoyalCustomer.
ecomm:placedOrder(?c, ?o1), ecomm:placedOrder(?c, ?o2), ecomm:placedOrder(?c, ?o3),
ecomm:placedOrder(?c, ?o4), ecomm:placedOrder(?c, ?o5), ecomm:placedOrder(?c, ?o6) 
→ ecomm:LoyalCustomer(?c)


If an order has a total price above 5000,
Then classify it as a HighValueOrder.
ecomm:totalPrice(?order, ?total), swrlb:greaterThan(?total, 5000) 
→ ecomm:HighValueOrder(?order)
